Privacy Policy – Subs No More

Last updated: 24 August 2025

Subs No More is a desktop application that helps users manage email subscriptions by scanning their Gmail account for unsubscribe links.

1. Information Collection and Use

Our application does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. All data processing occurs locally on the user’s device.

2. Google User Data

Subs No More uses the Gmail API only to read email headers and extract unsubscribe links. This access is solely for providing the unsubscribe functionality. No Google user data is shared with our servers, stored, or transferred to third parties.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or disclose user data to third parties under any circumstances.

4. Data Protection

All OAuth credentials and access tokens are securely stored locally on the user’s computer. We do not have access to these credentials. Users are responsible for protecting their own devices.

5. Revoking Access

Users may revoke the application’s access at any time via their Google Account Security settings: https://myaccount.google.com/permissions
