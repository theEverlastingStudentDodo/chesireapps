Privacy Policy – Subs No More CLI Tool

Subs No More is a local command-line application that helps users scan their Gmail inbox for unsubscribe links.

This application uses the Gmail API and requires users to authorize access via OAuth.

We do NOT:

  • Collect or transmit any data from your Gmail account
  • Store any emails or content on our servers (we don't have any servers)
  • Use tracking, telemetry, or analytics

What we DO:

  • Process emails locally on your machine
  • Use the Gmail read-only API scope for safe, limited access
  • Respect your privacy and your inbox
